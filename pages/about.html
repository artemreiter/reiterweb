About Me

Hi, I'm Artem Reiter

I'm a web analytics consultant with over 8 years of experience helping e-commerce businesses make data-driven decisions. Based in Europe, I work with clients worldwide, from fast-growing startups to established enterprises.

My journey in analytics started when I realized that most businesses were making marketing decisions based on gut feeling rather than data. I made it my mission to change that.

My Approach

I believe that analytics should be actionable, not just decorative. Every dashboard I create, every report I build, is designed to answer specific business questions and drive growth.

My clients see an average of 34% improvement in marketing ROI within the first 3 months of working together. But more importantly, they gain confidence in their decision-making.
